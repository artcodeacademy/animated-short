Animated Short

In this project we will create an animated movie with 3 scenes using code. Click in the example to view the final result:

1) Create a new project, choose 3 different backdrops for your scenes, and the objects and characters you want to include.

Hint: We reccomend using only 4 object to start, you can add more later on.

2) Now choose a backdrop and the object(s) for your first scene, when you press the green flag this is what you will see.

3) Make sure your objects start in the position you want and using code make them change positions, wait x secons, and/or turn, you decide because it's your movie!

Hint: Remeber that you can also add sounds, music, and dialogue.

4) Once you have your scene ready, we'll broadcast a message called "scene 2"

5) When the message is received make the backdrop change to the one you chose for your second scene, and make the objects from the first scene hide or to move to their next positions.

6) Add dialogue or movement, you can make your objects talk, think, or move around the scenary.

Hint: Be vigilant of the time, it will be necessary to use the wait block to make sure the actions happen at the right moment.

7) Repeat step 4 and call this message "scene 3".

8) Now repeat step 5 and develop your third scene when you receive the message.

Hint: Remember to hide the objects from the last scene that you will not use in the current scene.

Bonus: Add more scenes or make the scenes you already have more complex, remember you can use different blocks like glide, repeat, repat until, and that you can draw the objects and backdrops.

*** Important: Always remember to save your project and share it when you're done.
